    According to the stats there are almost 7 billion people in the world, out of which about 15-20% population of the world consists of disable persons (i.e., Blind, Color Blind, Motor impaired, Cognitive impaired). These 1 billion people have a spending power of $6 trillion which is a huge amount according to the business point of view. There are almost 1.92 billion websites in the world out which only 200 million (in Pakistan there are 13 million) are active now. And the figures are increasing day by day.

    The problem is that almost all the websites in the world are not fully accessible for all type of the impaired persons, which is a big problem for these disabled people. This issue must be solved so that these disabled people can also perform their work easily like other by using the online websites.

    The problem stated above is a big gap in the field of research as well as in the field of business. So, in this project idea we are going to remove this gap by making such a platform that will propose such a solution, each website in the world made fully accessible will for all type of impaired persons in the world.

  • 1. Screen Reader

    Sushi Software

    The Sushi Inventory Management System is a comprehensive software solution designed to address the unique inventory management needs of a sushi company. Developed using React, Node.js, Express API, and a SQL Database, the system offers a seamless way to efficiently manage inventory across multiple locations. Its user-friendly interface allows easy addition, modification, and removal of inventory items while providing real-time visibility into stock levels. A key feature is the automated low inventory alert system that promptly notifies administrators when stock levels fall below a predefined threshold. The intuitive admin panel empowers administrators to oversee inventory across various locations and take proactive measures to avoid stockouts. The system's secure user authentication, responsive design, and RESTful API integration ensure a smooth and secure user experience. By leveraging technology to enhance inventory control, streamline operations, and provide timely alerts.
